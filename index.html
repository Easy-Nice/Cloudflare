About

It's time to use Musicium in your server.

Musicium is an advanced Music Bot which allows you to play any type of Youtube Music song, or choose 35 Radio stations.
You can also attach filters, like Bassboost or Nightcore to peak the song quality to the next level!

    Info Commands:

    • support -- Gives you invite Link of the Support Server
    • invite -- Gives you an invite Link for Milrato
    • check -- Gives you every necessary Info about Milrato
    • uptime -- Gives you Milrato's Uptime
    • ping -- Gives you Milrato's Ping
    • feedback -- Sends us feedback!(30min cooldown)
    • request -- Request a command or anything!(5min cooldown)

    Music Commands:

    • filter <FILTERNAME> -- Set Audio - Effects
    • loop -- Toggle music loop
    • lyrics -- Get lyrics for the currently playing song
    • nowplaying -- Show current song
    • pause -- Pause the currently playing music
    • play <TITEL | URL> -- Plays song from YouTube/Stream
    • queue -- Show the music queue and now playing
    • radio <RADIOSTATION>-- Play a Radiostation
    • remove -- Remove song from the queue
    • resume -- Resume currently playing music
    • search <TITEL | URL>-- Search and select videos to play
    • shuffle -- Shuffles the current Queue!
    • skip -- Skip the currently playing song
    • skipto <QUEUE NUM.> -- Skip to the selected queue number
    • stop -- Stops the music
    • volume <VOL. NUM.> -- Change volume
    • help [COMMAND] -- Gives you a list of all help Commands

    General Commands:

    • help -- Gives you a List of all Commands
    • setup -- Setup a welcome message, ticket system, join to create System, or leveling/ranking System
    • vote -- Vote for Milrato!

    • userinfo -- Gives you Information of the User
    • avatar -- Gives you the avatar of the User
    • say -- Says a message
    • highlight -- Highlights a message
    • ascii -- Makes your message in ascii
    • quickembed -- Creates a quick custom Embed>>Follow steps
    • embed -- Creates an advanced custom Embed
    • announce -- Creates an announce Embed
    • yt -- Gives your all infos of a yt-vid
    • rank -- Get the Rank of a User
    • leaderboard -- Get the leaderboard of this Guild
